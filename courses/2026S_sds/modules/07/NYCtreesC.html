Carbon Storage, Tree growth and Caron Sequestration Rate for NYC Street Trees#

With NYC street tree species and dbh measurements, carbon storage for each tree can be calculated based on allometric equations. Half of he above-ground biomass (AGB) is carbon stored by trees. AGB = wood density * woody volume. Wood volume can be estimated as a function of dbh (or dbh and tree height) using allometric equations. Urban tree allometric equations (woody volume and wood density) can be found here.
