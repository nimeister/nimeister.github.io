## Overview
The course aims to introduce students to advanced topics in remote sensing applications, digital image processing and analysis. 
The course covers two main topics: one is using Google Earth Engine (GEE) – a cloud-based platform for large-scale geospatial analysis. 
Students are exposed to using machine learning and artificial intelligence for image classification and land use cover change analysis using GEE. 
The other is processing lidar data to extract topography and vegetation structure information using LASTool and FUSION software. 
Throughout the course, students will conduct a research project of environmental science applications using remote sensing data and communicate their findings. They will communicate their research results through oral presentations and writing research papers.
